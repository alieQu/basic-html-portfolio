My all-time Favourite Movies

Top 3 Movies Review

  1. Lilya 4-Ever

    Lilya Forever, I can say, is not for everyone. It's groundbreaking, bold, and quite disturbing.

    Besides all of that, it definitely is worth your time, especially if you are a young woman. I'm sure you are going to sympathize with the protagonist on another level

  2. Masculin Feminin directed by Jean-Luc Godard

    Masculin Feminin can be considered a cult film.

    If you set aside all the biases about Godard's cinema being boring, and realize that his movies appeal to a niche audience, you'll see it's a really great movie.

  3. Dancer in the Dark starring Björk

    Dancer in the Dark is one of my favourite movies. Both the actors and the director have a big role in why I liked this movie.

    The lead actor, Björk, delivered a great performance considering she actually is primarily a singer. And the director, Lars von Trier, made a great decision by making this movie a musical.

    I find his style very provocative and this is what makes it exciting to watch his movies. I wish I could forget it and watch it again. It would be just as captivating as it was the first time.
